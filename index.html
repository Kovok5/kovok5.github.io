A site built for skaters by a skater.

CONCRETE is a fan page dedicated to the world of skateboarding — from its origins in 1950s California to the technical street skating of today. Whether you just picked up your first board or have been pushing for years, there's something here for you.

Explore the history of skateboarding, figure out which deck setup is right for you, or study the tricks — from a basic ollie all the way up to the gnarliest flip variations. I tried to summarize everything simply and honestly. There will be no sponsorships or ads, this is a passion project.

Hope this will be helpful. Go SK8.
